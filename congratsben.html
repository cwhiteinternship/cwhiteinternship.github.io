CONGRATULATIONS

I created a banner of congratulations for a high school graduation. His high school colors were dark blue, and the college he would be attending in the fall also used dark blue and light blue. I chose to use a decorative script font with the headline “Congratulations Ben” but for the rest of the text, I used a serif type face. I decided to incorporate the name of the high school and his future college as well as the college’s logo. Between the name of the high school and college, I added “Class of 2019”. Around the congratulatory section of the banner, I used an outline to emphasize the purpose of the banner.

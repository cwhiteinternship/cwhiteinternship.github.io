CARLOZZI

A project I worked on today was lettering a trailer. A coworker and I were given a paper showing where the design should be located. First, we wiped that area of the trailer with alcohol to remove dirt and dust. After we eyeballed the location of the vinyl, we taped the vinyl with its backing to the trailer. Then we measured and adjusted until we found the exact location. Once, it was in the correct location, we peeled the backing off, squeegeed it down, and removed the top layer of tape. We repeated this process on each side of the trailer.

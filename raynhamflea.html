RAYNHAM FLEA MARKET

I designed two things for the Raynham Flea Market. The first project was creating a code of conduct sign. They had seen one they liked but wanted us to reproduce it with their colors and a few minor changes. I added the flea markets mascot for an additional touch. They also wanted parking signs. My job was to design a few different options with their mascot and colors. We sent them to the client and are waiting to hear their feedback.

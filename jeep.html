JEEP MAGNET X

I really enjoyed working on Jeep magnets for breast cancer and autism awareness. My boss works with a company called Jeep Magnet X, and they create magnets for the sides of Jeeps so they don’t get scratched when going in the woods. Although my boss doesn’t take his Jeep off roading, it is a great way to change the look of his Jeep and some of the designs have meaning behind them. What I did was create a pink breast cancer design and mask it into the Jeep magnets so you could see exactly how it would look applied on the Jeep. I repeated this when working on the autism awareness magnets. For the autism design, I made one set with large puzzle pieces and one smaller.

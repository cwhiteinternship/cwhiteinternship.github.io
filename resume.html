About Me

Hi, I'm Casey White. I have lived on Cape Cod all of my life. I am a quiet person but have a strong design style and am very motivated. Growing up, I was on an IEP. It was hard to take tests, I was unable to comprehend anything, and inevitably I felt stupid. But in sophomore year of high school, I chose to take yearbook. I thought it was just going to be another boring elective, not something that I would want to do for the rest of my life. I felt like I had a purpose, designing was fun, it was my passion. Working with photographs, copy, and other elements, to create something as a whole was very satisfying.
